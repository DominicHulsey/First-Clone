How to Business:

A 3-step guide to guaranteed overnight success

1. Look the part

As the saying goes, business is 95% looking the part. When it comes to the modern business environment, there is no such thing as overdressing. Don't believe us? Just look at this picture of Elon Musk and Peter Thiel from when they founded paypal. Today, those two have a combined net worth of 23 billion dollars. You do the math.

2. Assert your dominance

Letting others know that you are in charge is another crucial step to achieving business success. When in business meetings, our experts recommend that you make sure that your voice is at least 10 decibels louder than anyone else in the room. A similar approach should be taken when it comes to your handshake; make sure to always double (or even triple) the grip strength being used by the other person. This sends a signal to your fellow businessmen that if any deal is going to be made, it will only be made on your terms.

3. Start a business

If you have been following along with the first two steps that we've outlined, this last step shouldn't be too difficult, so we won't go into much detail. First, find a product that you want to sell. Then, sell it for more than it costs you. Not that complicated.
